## Week 3 Progress

During the third week of the internship, we started working on a dataset provided by our mentor as a team activity. The purpose of this task was to apply the concepts and tools we had learned in the previous weeks in a more practical way.

As a team, we explored the dataset and tried to understand its structure and the information it contained. We analyzed different columns in the dataset and discussed how the data could be cleaned and prepared before performing any analysis.

During this process, we used Power BI and Power Query to examine the dataset and apply the data cleaning techniques we had learned earlier. This included checking for missing values, duplicate records, incorrect data types, and possible errors in the data.

Working in a team also helped us share ideas and approaches for handling the dataset, which made the learning experience more interactive. It allowed us to better understand how collaboration plays an important role when working on real-world data projects.

Overall, this week helped me apply the concepts of data preparation, data cleaning, and dataset exploration in a practical setting while also gaining experience working collaboratively with others.
